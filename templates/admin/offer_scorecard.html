Proxy, not a causal link. There is no offer→sale relationship in the data, so this attributes all payment revenue on a project recorded after its offer was sent — not just incremental print/album upsell. Project-level aggregation avoids double-counting a shared payment; offers on galleries with no linked project aren't attributable here. Read it as a directional signal alongside the COGS in AI cost.

This scorecard only reports. Whether the offers capability is kept or retired (audit §19.4) is a human call — a healthy approval rate and real pipeline value argue keep; a long run of proposed-but-never-approved offers argues retire.
